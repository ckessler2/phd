**How to build an Alsomitra Microflier**
---
**Required materials and tools:**
* Flier base (3.6mm Mylar)
* Carbon rod (0.25mm diameter)
* Glue (BSI maxi-cure extra thick)
  
* Structural jig & 14 alignment washers (3D printed plastic)
* 14 Alignment pins (1.6mm)

**Manufacturing steps:**
1. Prepare materials
2. Prepare tools
3. Assemble materials in jig
4. Align rod to edge
5. Glue rod to base, wait until it sets
6. Dissasemble jig, clean up flier
